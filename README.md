
# Hello, I'm Yufei. 
I've been interested in the vividness of the living world and how we can interact with it through physical models and community knowledge making.

I grew roots for this interest from living in and getting to know people and the land in Oʻahu. I then gained the tookbox for complex systems at Minerva University where I studied computational and math modeling. 

To learn more about the experiment and theory side, I've apprenticed with Prof. Mark Peifer (UNC; [poster](https://file.notion.so/f/f/5f96d4fa-63ff-4b81-8256-928a27bb0ffc/73a09089-8414-4590-ad3d-911d71c055d5/Untitled.pdf?id=20ded5fe-f738-4ef5-9d88-17d3ce5aeda2&table=block&spaceId=5f96d4fa-63ff-4b81-8256-928a27bb0ffc&expirationTimestamp=1719928800000&signature=JJoX11cOrYKKNJYN4SVB6DaygqymgdAwJzbiPI3X0Ks&downloadName=Untitled.pdf)), Exa.TrkX group (FermiLab; [poster](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1Dddsb7ufFjb7TCKAGfvXUI3oDRWeM89lE6LiBh47aKQ/edit#slide=id.g1478c9558b0_0_56)), collective information processing group (Humboldt Universitat; [thesis](https://docs.google.com/document/d/18DW1_5gIHG7N3sYjLHdN2yCImmQQzQo-fRJQHNIIBX4/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.umk2p526ldqw)), interactive structures group (CMU; [poster](https://file.notion.so/f/f/f944ed2b-2d16-4ae6-9236-95cf0922e329/176364b5-74f0-40ec-94e8-59b1e8c44dd2/REU_Poster_(Yufei_Xiao).pdf?id=82e4547d-0fb9-492f-98b6-02c53825e2b7&table=block&spaceId=f944ed2b-2d16-4ae6-9236-95cf0922e329&expirationTimestamp=1719950400000&signature=ocBXqmJo5Q2nzoh2H9fTYCDkLAkY-IODLKt24t03Nog&downloadName=REU+Poster+%28Yufei+Xiao%29.pdf)). 

To learn more about the relationship and action side, I've stayed with [Colectivo Reciclador](https://www.notion.so/elianna/Waste-at-Anita-Brocoli-d68b9f290a5843eebd4684af5c974f0c), a community farm in Buenos Aires; [Blackberry Ridge](https://xyfei.notion.site/Blackberry-Ridge-Farm-e74078e391a44e7a9a6ef9d134e91968), sustainable construction in Vermont; and [Lullaby Search project](https://graphfei.substack.com/p/love-songs), an oral history and song revival team in Yunnan, China. I also tried to learn biomimicry through making [a wetland flood filter](https://www.notion.so/xyfei/Wetland-Filter-2bd734e271c44fc7a49334d57327fa7f) and [particle swarm optimization](https://www.notion.so/xyfei/Particle-Swarm-Optimization-d295e0ac83ed442d895eaa1ad6b5d58b?pvs=25). I've also been playing with how [diagrams, comics and drawing together](https://xyfei.notion.site/Shapes-54fef1a4ced34a609631806011d0114b?pvs=4) can come into this.
