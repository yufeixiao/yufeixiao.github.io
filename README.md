# Portfolio | Yufei Xiao
## Place-Based Projects
#### Bay
Modeled the effect of sea-level rise on urban flooding risk using the NOAA Surging Seas projections.

Analyzed the relationship between coastline proximity and flooding potential in San Francisco neighborhoods using the Community Resilience Index.

Conducted problem and gap analysis on San Francisco groundwater storage against Californian Droughts.

#### Extraterrestrial
Programmed a simple angular reconstruction tool for low-energy cosmic ray particle showers.

Used EM Mechanics instead of GR to model inspiral gravitational waves to understand it a little more.

Created 3D objects inspired by black hole structure and dynamics: a fabric two-finger ring as BH merger, and an amber with embedded BH.

Mapped out a science fiction story premise and skeleton answering: "What if solar geoengineering goes wrong?"

#### Ala Wai
Restored watershed by removing invasive catfish and growing native plants.

Regularly monitored water quality using YSI Probe and Photometer.

Tested Regional Ocean Modeling System with physical oceanography data.

#### Yellow Mountain
Hiked to observe and sketch 2 sketchbooks worth of rocks, from detail to general orogeny.

->One year later, synthesized sketches and traditional extreme dimension scrolls to reconstruct a new landscape.

Visited multiple Buddhist cave temples carved on mountainsides to mimic the statues and murals in paintings.

->Three years later, designed and built urban cave-like structures repurposed for meditation and play.
